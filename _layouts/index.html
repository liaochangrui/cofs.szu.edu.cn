Nanophotonics group is in the College of Optical Science and Engineering, and the State Key Laboratory of Modern Optical Instrumentation at Zhejiang University. Our group explores the science, technology and art of light on the nanoscale. Our research interests include calculation, fabrication, manipulation, characterization and functionalization of low-dimensional photonic structures for both scientific research and technological applications.
